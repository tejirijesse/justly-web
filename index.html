01 — The Problem

The justice gap is not a funding problem.
It is an infrastructure problem.

Courts exist. Lawyers exist. Laws exist. But for most people in Nigeria, Kenya, Ghana, Uganda, and Rwanda — the system is inaccessible in language, cost, geography, and design. One hour with a lawyer costs more than a week of wages. Legal processes are built for professionals, not people. And the gap keeps growing.

Infrastructure fixes that. We are building the infrastructure.

02 — The Platform

Four layers. One infrastructure.
Zero friction.

Justly is not a chatbot. It does not give generic advice. It builds your case — from first message to legal resolution.

AI Legal Intelligence

Jurisdiction-specific legal guidance across Nigeria, Kenya, Ghana, Uganda, and Rwanda. Plain language. Instant. Calibrated to your exact situation — not a general answer.

Encrypted Evidence Vault

Every photo, document, audio file, and video you submit is encrypted, timestamped, and chain-of-custody verified. It holds up in court. That is the point.

Verified Lawyer Network

Matched to verified counsel with your full case already prepared. No cold calls. No wasted consultations. No starting from zero.

Case Management

Every deadline, document, update, and next action in one place. For individuals, lawyers, and institutions — from first incident to final resolution.
